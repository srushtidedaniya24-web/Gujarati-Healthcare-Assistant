Challenges & Learnings

Reflecting on the journey, obstacles overcome, and insights gained while building the Gujarati Healthcare Assistant.

warning Challenges
Data Scarcity
Limited availability of Gujarati clinical corpora required extensive manual annotation and synthetic data generation.
Morphological Complexity
Gujarati's agglutinative nature challenged standard NLP pipelines, requiring custom tokenization and subword modeling.
Clinical Accuracy
Medical translation demands near-perfect precision; even minor errors can have serious consequences for patient safety.
emoji_objects Key Learnings
Subword Modeling is Essential
FastText's subword approach outperformed word-level models for Gujarati's rich morphology and compound formation.
Domain Adaptation Matters
Pre-trained multilingual models required significant domain-specific fine-tuning to handle medical terminology accurately.
Human-in-the-Loop Validation
Clinical deployment necessitated clinician verification loops — pure AI automation was insufficient for high-stakes medical use.
