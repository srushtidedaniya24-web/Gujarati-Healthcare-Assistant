From Gujarati Query to Clinical Insight

The Gujarati Healthcare Chatbot processes patient queries through 17 steps across 5 phases — from raw text preprocessing (Segmentation → Tokenization → Noise Removal → Text Cleaning), through Feature Engineering (N-Grams → BoW → TF-IDF), Language Representation (Word Embeddings → Contextual Embeddings), Language Models (NLM → Transformer), to Symptom Detection, Chatbot Response, Gujarati Translation, and Patient Delivery.

✂️ Segmentation 🪙 Tokenization 🧹 Noise Removal 🧼 Text Cleaning 🌍 Translation 📖 N-Grams 👜 BoW 📊 TF-IDF 🧠 Word Emb. 🌐 Contextual 🤖 NLM 🚀 Transformer 🩺 Symptom Det. 💊 Response 📱 Delivery
